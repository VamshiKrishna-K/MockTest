Instructions

Please read the instructions carefully

General Instructions:

  1. Total duration of examination is 150 minutes.
  2. The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer will display the remaining time. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end automatically.
  3. The Question Palette displayed on the right side of screen will show the status of each question using the following symbols:
    1. You have not visited the question yet.
    2. You have not answered the question.
    3. You have answered the question.
    4. You have NOT answered the question, but marked for review.
    5. Answered and Marked for Review will be evaluated.
  4. The Marked for Review status indicates you want to revisit the question.
  5. You can collapse or expand the question palette using arrows.
  6. You can navigate using scroll buttons without scrolling.

Navigating to a Question:

  • Click on the question number to go directly.
  • Click on Save & Next to save your answer.
  • Click on Mark for Review & Next to review later.

Answering a Question:

  • Select an option by clicking on it.
  • Click again to deselect.
  • Use Clear Response to remove answer.
  • You must click Save & Next to save.

Navigating through sections:

  • Sections appear at the top of the screen.
  • You can switch sections anytime.
  • Section summary appears above the question palette.
  • Hover over images to zoom.
